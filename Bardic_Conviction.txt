====== Bardic Conviction ======
Created Sunday 16 August 2020

Power coming from some sort of vision

Once per long rest, when you use [[:Bardic Inspiration]] , you may expend any number of dice to empower that many allies.
Any allies you empower this way will always land their next attack.
